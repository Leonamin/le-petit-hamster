# Visual Philosophy

The world should feel larger than life.

Players should feel like a small traveler
walking through a world that was not built for them.

The goal is not realism.

The goal is to recreate how a hamster
might emotionally perceive the world.

# Scale

The world is viewed from the perspective
of a small rodent.

Objects should appear larger and more significant
than they would from a human perspective.

Examples:

Flower Pot = Small Forest
Bench = Shelter
Mailbox = Tower
Drain Pipe = Cave

# Camera

Third Person

Low Perspective

Camera should remain close to the ground.

Players should often look slightly upward
towards the world.

# Planet Design

Each planet is a miniature diorama.

Players should be able to walk around
an entire planet within a few minutes.

Every planet should be memorable.

No procedural generation.

# Mood

Wonder
Curiosity
Warmth
Solitude
Friendship
Gentle Melancholy

# Avoid

Dark Fantasy
Hyper Realism
Military Themes
Excessive UI
Crowded Worlds
Competitive Design
